## 🧭 Who We Are

Huazhi Machinery is a professional manufacturer of metal shredders and scrap steel crushers. Our main products include metal shredders, scrap steel crushers, bicycle shredders, metal crushers, and car body crushers, among other metal crushing and shredding equipment. We also offer specialized production line configurations for metal shredders and scrap steel crushers. Our series of metal shredders and scrap steel crushers feature innovative designs, delivering high crushing efficiency and high output.
